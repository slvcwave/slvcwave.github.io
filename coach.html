Wayne is the SLVC WAVE Club Coordinator and has been coaching consecutively for the last eighteen years doing multiple teams a season, and every age group from 13U to 18U. Wayne has coached eight years at HJ Cody High School, JV/SR Girls from 2001 to 2003, SR Boys 2013-2015 and JV Girls in 2016 and 2017. Wayne has been coaching beach volleyball for the last twelve years and was the Zone 4 coach at the last four Alberta Summer Games. Wayne started coaching in Fort McMurray in 1991 with grade five and six boys volleyball.

Wayne's philosophy is to create strong individuals who believe in themselves, and to give players an opportunity to achieve individual success which will translate into a positive team experience in victory or defeat. Wayne believes this can be achieved through hard work, patience, communication, respect, setting goals and enjoying the moment.

Caroline plays in a women's league in Red Deer and has found a passion with coaching. Caroline's philosophy is working hard and having fun. Teaching accountability and respecting your teammates it also very important. "Extremely excited to be back with the Wave again this season!"
