2018 Try-Outs

REGISTER WITH
VOLLEYBALL ALBERTA

Volleyball Alberta requires that EVERY player trying out MUST register online as a 2018 Youth Player - Recreational  (around $8) for insurance purposes. Proof of purchase must be shown before entering our gyms. 

SLVC WAVE
TRY-OUT FORM

Age criteria is listed on our Club Try-Out form. Please note that if you are trying out for multiple teams (playing up), we require that you attend both team's try-out. Bring your completed form and our one-time $10 fee to first try-out. Dates/times will be listed in our calendar.

PLAYING UP
INFO SHEET

If you are unsure on what age group your daughter would fit best, please read our info page about 'Playing Up'.  

TEAM PLAYER
WAIVER FORM

Players picked for a team MUST attend our signing/jersey fitting meeting. Bring your signed Volleyball Alberta Waiver/Club Commitment form with a non-refundable $250 deposit. 

MEDICAL FORM

Bring to first practice in January. All of our Club forms are fillable if opened with Adobe Acrobat (free app).

PARENT CODE OF CONDUCT FORM

Please sign and bring to the parent meeting.  

PARENT INFO SHEET

Our Parent Info sheet explains a bit about our Club, fees, and expectations for the season. We will discuss all this in greater detail at the parent meeting.

SPONSORSHIP FORM

Sponsorship goes directly to reducing team fees. If you happen to work for (or own) a company that would be interested, our Sponsorship letter is linked above.
