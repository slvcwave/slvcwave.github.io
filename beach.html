SLVC Beach

It's finally summer!  Wayne will start setting up our beach nets down on the pier. We run practices once a week for all Club players who want to learn the game and prepare for the Volleyball Alberta Youth Beach Tournaments. Parents are responsible for registering, paying, and driving to all of the tournaments. Please read the handbook and player officiating pdfs, found in the link below.

Age
Categories

Our practices are for athletes who have played at least one year of Club Volleyball.  Age categories for the Volleyball Alberta tournaments start at 14U, and more info can be found on their website.  Please read all the handbooks so you are familiar with the new rules, format and player expectations.

5/16/2018

Weather-Related
Cancellations

Practice cancellations will be posted in our Club Calendar, one hour before start time. Don't forget your bugspray, sunscreen, water, sunglasses and socks (sand can get hot), and we'll see you at the pier!
