 SLVC WAVE HISTORY

Our Club started way back in 2000 with two 'we-hope-enough-show-up!' girl's teams. We now host multiple try-outs for all six age groups (13U to 18U), with players attending from all over Central Alberta. We are a non-profit organization, just volunteers that really, really like volleyball and coaching. We do our best to ensure our players a memorable volleyball experience, with skilled coaching (seriously, check out their bios), tons of practice time and competitive game play.

practice & Play

Our Club practices Tuesday & Thursday evenings, plus Sunday afternoons. We are based out of the Sylvan Lake Multi-Campus, which is big enough to fit all our teams under one roof. Club season starts with try-outs in December and ends in April (May for the older teams). We enter all the Volleyball Alberta Premier Tournaments which are held on Saturdays & Sundays in Edmonton or Calgary. We also arrange exhibitions and enter private tournaments throughout the Club season. You will find all these events listed in our Club Calendar.

fees

Each player's fees are comprised of tournament entry costs, Volleyball Alberta registration fees, coach expenses, gym rentals and athlete clothing packages. Totals depend on how many tournaments are entered and how many players are on the team. Typically they range from $600 at the 13U level to $1200 for the older girls. Hotels and travelling expenses for players and parents are extra. We welcome team sponsors, their donations help tremendously in reducing the costs for parents.
