Meet a few of Our Coaches

Wayne Halliday

Club Coordinator/Head Coach

Wayne started coaching in Sylvan Lake in 2000, and hasn't slowed down since. Typically he starts in September coaching at HJ Cody, then runs his Club and coaches (sometimes two teams) from December to May. June finds him on the pier setting up the beach nets. Every other year in July,  he's at the Alberta Summer Games. Yes, he does have a real job and family.  
AND HE HAS A BOBBLEHEAD!

Rick Boyes

Head Coach

Rick (guy on the right) hanging with Glenn Hoag at a recent coach clinic. Rick constantly strives to up his coaching game and it shows. Three athletes from his 2017 18U team have signed with post secondary schools this fall! Like Wayne, he also has a real job and a family, who either coach or play(ed) for our Club. Rick also has a super successful gig coaching school volleyball every fall.
AND HE HAS A BRICK!    

Rhonda Carpenter

Head Coach

This picture demonstrates why these two coaches are never allowed to leave our Club! Each year they choose twelve girls then blast them with three months of everything volleyball. And by the end of the season? The whole team will show growth, positivity and mad skills. Guaranteed.
